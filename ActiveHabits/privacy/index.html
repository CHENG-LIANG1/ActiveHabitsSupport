Introduction

ActiveHabits ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

All data you create, including habits and logs, is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on our servers.

Local Storage

Your data is stored using SwiftData. No data leaves your device unless you choose to back it up through Apple's iCloud services.

No Third-Party Services

ActiveHabits does not integrate with any third-party analytics or advertising services.
