About ActiveHabits

ActiveHabits is a beautiful and intuitive habit tracking app designed to help you build consistent daily routines. With features like streak tracking, detailed analytics, achievements, and customizable themes, staying on top of your habits has never been easier.

Key Features

  • Simple Habit Logging - One-tap logging with haptic feedback
  • Streak Tracking - Monitor your consistency with streak counters
  • Analytics & Insights - Weekly, monthly, and yearly statistics with heatmaps
  • Achievement System - Unlock 50+ achievements across multiple tiers
  • Beautiful Themes - 20+ themes including developer favorites
  • Widgets - Quick access from your home screen
  • Privacy First - All data stored locally on your device

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my data synced to the cloud?

A: No. ActiveHabits stores all data locally on your device for maximum privacy. Your habits and logs never leave your device.

Q: How many habits can I track?

A: You can track up to 20 habits simultaneously.

Q: Can I export my data?

A: Data export functionality is coming in a future update.

Q: How do I unlock achievements?

A: Achievements are unlocked automatically as you log habits. Keep building streaks and completing habits to discover all achievements!
