Common Questions

How to start a journey?

Select your preferred transportation mode and focus duration on the Setup screen, then tap "Start Journey" to begin your virtual travel.

What are POIs?

POIs (Points of Interest) are landmarks you discover during your focus session. The further you "travel", the more unique places you'll find.

How to switch UI styles?

Go to "Settings" to toggle between "Liquid Glass" and "Neomorphic" visual styles to suit your focus environment.

How to save my journey summary?

At the end of a session, tap the "Save" icon in the Journey Summary view to export a beautiful record to your Photo Library.

Troubleshooting

  • Journey not progressing: Ensure Location Services are set to "While Using" in iOS Settings so the app can calculate your virtual movement.
  • Weather not updating: Check your internet connection. Roam Focus uses WeatherKit to sync real-time conditions.
  • Missing summary image: Please ensure you've granted "Add Photos Only" permission to save images.

常见问题

如何开启一段旅程？

在设置页面选择您偏好的运输模式和专注时长，点击“开始旅程”即可进入虚拟环球旅行。

什么是 POI（兴趣点）？

POI 是您在专注期间发现的地标建筑。随着专注时间的增加，您的虚拟角色会“行进”得更远，从而解锁更多独特的地点。

如何切换视觉风格？

在“设置”中，您可以随心切换“液态玻璃 (Liquid Glass)”或“拟态 (Neomorphic)”风格，以匹配您的专注氛围。

如何保存旅程总结？

专注结束后，在旅程总结页面点击“保存”图标，即可将精美的旅程卡片导出至系统相册。

故障排查

  • 旅程进度没有更新： 请在系统“设置”中确保 Roam Focus 的位置权限已设置为“使用期间允许”，以便应用模拟虚拟位移。
  • 天气信息显示异常： 请检查您的网络连接。应用通过 WeatherKit 同步实时天气环境。
  • 无法保存图片： 请确保已授予应用“仅添加照片”的权限。
