Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 12, 2026

Our Privacy Philosophy

Roam Focus is a local-first productivity application designed for iOS. We believe that your focus time and journey data are deeply personal and should remain under your exclusive control.

Data Storage & Sync

All your focus sessions, journey records, and discovered landmarks are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. We do not operate any servers to store your personal activity data.

Permissions Usage

Location Services: We request location access to simulate your virtual journey based on real-world movement and to help you discover nearby landmarks during focus sessions. This data is processed locally and never uploaded.

Photo Library: Required only for the "Save Journey Summary" function. We do not access, read, or scan your existing photo collection.

Third-Party Services

WeatherKit: We use Apple's WeatherKit to provide real-time weather context for your journeys. This is handled via Apple's privacy-preserving APIs.

No Tracking: We do not integrate any third-party analytics (like Firebase or Mixpanel) or advertising SDKs. Your app usage patterns are never monitored.

隐私理念

Roam Focus 是一款面向 iOS 平台的“本地优先”专注应用。我们深信，您的专注时长和旅程数据属于高度隐私，应当始终由您本人掌控。

数据存储与同步

您的所有专注会话、旅程记录和发现的地标均通过 SwiftData 存储在您的设备本地。我们不运营任何服务器来存储您的个人活动数据。

权限说明

地理位置权限： 我们请求位置访问权限，旨在根据您的实际位移模拟虚拟旅程，并在专注期间帮助您发现附近的地标。此类数据仅在本地处理，绝不上传。

照片库权限： 仅在您执行“保存旅程总结图”操作时使用。我们不会访问、读取或扫描您相册中的已有内容。

第三方服务

WeatherKit： 我们集成 Apple 的 WeatherKit 为您的旅程提供实时天气环境。该服务遵循 Apple 的隐私保护 API 规范。

无追踪： 应用内未集成任何第三方分析工具（如 Firebase 或 Mixpanel）或广告 SDK。我们不会监控您的任何应用使用习惯。
